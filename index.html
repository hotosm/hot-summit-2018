HOT Summit 2018

Dar es Salaam, Tanzania - August 31, 2018

On August 31, HOT will convene for its fourth annual global community meeting. The entire HOT community will come together to showcase projects, debate and share ideas, and learn how mapping projects can have impact on the lives beyond the people mapping. This year's summit is being held in partnership with FOSS4G 2018 for the first time in Africa. Read more below about the program and register for FOSS4G to attend.
